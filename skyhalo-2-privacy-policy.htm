Information Collected while Using the Application

  • Information regarding your location

While using Our Application, SkyHalo™ 2 (for obvious reasons) requires access to your location. This may be done automatically through the use of your watch / phone's GPS, or you may leave these sensors turned off and manually provide a specific location to the application for which you would like a forecast. This information is never sent to us, nor reported in any way.

SkyHalo™ 2 uses this information to provide forecasts and alerts to your device. The information is uploaded to, and may be stored on, a weather provider's servers, or it may be simply stored on Your device.

You can enable or disable access to this information at any time, through Your Device settings.
